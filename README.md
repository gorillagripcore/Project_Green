## What is this?

Project Green is for anyone who'd rather write a quick PowerShell script than trust a $4 Amazon mouse jiggler of mysterious origin. Keep your screen awake, your status green, and your IT department none the wiser.

| Part | What it does |
|------|-------------|
| **DVD Screen Waker** | A bouncing dot animation in the browser. Uses the Screen Wake Lock API to prevent your screen from sleeping. |
| **Cursor "Animation"** | A beginner PowerShell exercise that just happens to move your mouse in a smooth circle every 30ms. Totally for learning purposes. Also, keeps Teams from going yellow. |
